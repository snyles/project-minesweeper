  • Left Click to uncover a square.
  • The numbers in the uncovered squares tell you how many mines are next to that square.
  • Right Click to mark a square with a flag if you think it contains a mine.
  • Double Click or Click Both Mouse Buttons on a numbered square once you have marked all the mines around it. This will reveal the surrounding squares.
  • Mark all the mines with a flag to win!
